About the Game

Reef Bloom is a free browser-based ecosystem game where you build and nurture your own coral reef. Place coral on a grid, hatch fish that swim freely through your reef, and watch your underwater world grow more vibrant over time. There is no failure state and no time pressure — you are a steward, not a controller. Reef Bloom is designed as a calm, creative experience that rewards patience and curiosity.

How to Play

  • Place coral on the 10×10 grid to generate Bubble Energy (BE), the in-game currency that powers your reef.
  • Hatch fish by selecting a species and tapping an open tile. Fish swim autonomously using boids-style flocking AI.
  • Grow your Harmony — as you add diverse species, your reef's Harmony score rises and the world becomes more colorful and saturated.
  • Level up to unlock new species across Common, Rare, and Epic tiers — from clownfish and chromis to dolphins and sharks.
  • Explore biomes — travel between Coral Reef, Seagrass Basin, and Deep Twilight, each with unique species and mechanics.
  • Step away and earn — idle streak bonuses reward you with bonus Bubble Energy when you return after a break.

Features

  • 20+ species — coral and fish with procedurally generated visuals, each looking slightly different every time
  • Multiple biomes — Coral Reef for classic reef-building, Seagrass Basin with grazer mechanics, and Deep Twilight with bioluminescent creatures
  • Seasonal events — limited-time events with exclusive species, daily quests, and collectible clams
  • Three save slots — keep multiple reefs going at once, each with its own progress and biomes
  • No accounts required — your data stays in your browser via localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server
  • Works on desktop and tablet — landscape-oriented design built with PixiJS for smooth, responsive gameplay

🌿 SEAGRASS BASIN

Environment: Shallow (1–5 m)

Dense seagrass meadows with sandy patches and occasional rocks. Grazers generate bonus Bubble Energy while feeding. Dual-biome fish explore freely between Coral Reef and Seagrass Basin.

Key Mechanics

  • Bubble Energy: Standard coral BE applies. Grazers generate +1 to +4 additional BE per tick depending on rarity.
  • Harmony: Biodiversity slightly increases Harmony. Dual-biome fish grazing in seagrass provide small harmony boosts.
  • Grazers swim on Layer A, blocked only by tall coral or rocks. Seagrass patches occupy 1×1 tiles and ripple visually when grazed.
  • Unlock: Grazer species unlock via seagrass patch placement or observation. Dual-biome fish explore automatically once unlocked in Coral Reef.

Grazer BE Bonuses

  • 🟪 Uncommon → +1 BE / tick
  • 🟦 Rare → +2 BE / tick
  • 🟩 Super Rare → +3 BE / tick
  • 🟨 Epic → +4 BE / tick
